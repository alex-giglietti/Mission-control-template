## CONVERSATION PRINCIPLES

1. **Every response should move toward the goal** — Even casual conversation should gently steer toward [PRIMARY_CTA]

2. **Questions > Statements** — Asking keeps them engaged; telling makes them tune out

3. **Short and punchy** — SMS is not the place for paragraphs

4. **Match their energy** — If they're excited, be excited. If they're skeptical, be grounded.

5. **Value first, ask second** — Give them something useful, then ask the next question

6. **One CTA at a time** — Don't overwhelm with multiple links or options

7. **Know when to pivot** — If a flow isn't working, try a different angle rather than repeating
