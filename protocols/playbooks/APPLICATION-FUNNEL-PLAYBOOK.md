## Overview

### What is the Application Funnel?

The Application Funnel is a simplified, low-friction alternative to traditional funnels (VSL, Webinar, Group) that gets prospects on sales calls faster with less tech overhead.

**Funnel Flow:**
```
Offer Ad → Application → Booking Page → [OPTIONAL: TY Page → HW Page] → Sales Call
```

### Why Use It?

- ✅ Bypasses most tech setup
- ✅ Less friction to book calls
- ✅ No VSL needed for initial validation (especially to get to $100-200k/month)
- ✅ Easy to manage (no CRM required initially, though highly recommended)

---

## Who Should Use This

### ✅ Use Cases
- First validating your offer
- First validating paid traffic
- Need FAST low-tech implementation with minimal media buying skills

**Note:** Great to test BEFORE or alongside a VSL. VSLs require more copywriting/presentation skills, video editing, and budget.

### ❌ When NOT to Use
- **Smaller TAM, B2B enterprise markets** (TAM below 10k)
- **If you already have a VSL/Webinar working** - Review with your AM first to avoid "shiny object syndrome"

## Writing Your Ads

### Direct vs Indirect Offers

#### Direct Offers
- **Format:** `(Target Audience) - we'll (outcome) in (timeframe) or (guarantee)`
- **Example:** "Coaches & Agencies: We'll Place You With A Proven Salesperson And If They Don't Perform Within 30 Days - You Don't Pay!"
- ✅ Easier to execute
- ✅ Works well for B2B/B2Solopreneur

#### Indirect Offers
- Requires stronger copywriting skills
- Creates an "open loop" around their problem/goal
- Needs a strong **Unique Mechanism**
- ✅ Works exceptionally well for B2C (health, relationships)

**Recommended trainings:**
- Proven Ad Angles (B2C Section)
- Copy Platform
- Market Research
- Indirect VSL Ads
- Unique Mechanisms w/ Todd Brown

#### Indirect Hook → Direct Offer (Bonus)
- Best of both worlds
- Uses superior copywriting to hook leads
- Solidifies quality with a guarantee

### Which Should You Use?

**General rule:**
- **B2B & B2Solopreneur:** Primarily DIRECT
- **B2C:** Primarily INDIRECT

**Recommendation:** Write BOTH. Even if you only use one, it makes you a better copywriter.

For B2C: Write at least TWO Indirect Ads.

### Option A: Full Script

1. **Call-Out**
2. **State Offer**
3. **Expand w/ 3 Key Results**
4. **Restate Guarantee**

## Indirect Offer Ad Framework

### Basic Outline

```
Hook → "Daggers" → Unique Mechanism → Social Proof → CTA
```

---

### 4 Basic Hooks

#### 1. Secret
```
This ONE thing [dramatic result]. Want to know what it is? 

Well, first, let me tell you what it's NOT…
```

**Examples:**
- "This ONE thing saved my marriage from divorce and completely turned our relationship around. Want to know what it is?"
- "What's the best funnel for coaches/consultants in 2023?"

#### 2. Story
```
How did I [transformation] in [timeframe]? 

Well, it wasn't by…
```

**Examples:**
- "How did I go from a broke bartender to making more than a neurosurgeon in 12 months?"
- "How did this woman drop 3 jean sizes eating donuts every day?"

#### 3. Promise
```
Want to know how I [impressive result]?

Well, it wasn't by…
```

**Examples:**
- "Want to know how I scaled a $79 million coaching business in just 3 years?"
- "Want to know how I got this girl that was WAY out of my league to marry me?"

#### 4. Problem (Semi-Direct)
```
[Pain point question]? 

Well, a LOT has changed…
```

**Examples:**
- "Struggling to get leads for your coaching business in 2023?"
- "Want to publish a book but don't have the time?"

---

### "Daggers" Section

After the hook, throw "daggers" at common solutions/mistakes to set up your unique mechanism.

**Example:**

```
It's NOT:

❌ Amazon FBA
❌ Dropshipping
❌ Becoming a coach or consultant
❌ Starting a digital marketing agency
❌ Affiliate marketing
❌ RE, crypto…

All of those businesses take way too much time and money to set up and usually take over a year to actually make any profits.
```

**More Dagger Examples:**

**Fitness:**
- ❌ Keto
- ❌ Intermittent Fasting
- ❌ Starving Yourself
- ❌ Risky Weight-Loss Medications

**Marriage Coaching:**
- ❌ Couples Counseling
- ❌ Therapy
- ❌ Church Pastors / Leaders
- ❌ Books & Podcasts

**Lead Gen:**
- ❌ Facebook Ads
- ❌ YouTube Ads
- ❌ TikTok
- ❌ SEO

### Unique Mechanism

**⚠️ Watch Todd Brown's training before writing this section.**

**Outline:** `Introduce Mechanism → QUICK Explanation (1-4 sentences) → Benefits`

**Example:**

```
It's called Remote Closing. Here's how it works…

There are thousands of online businesses that sell products or services for anywhere from $5-15k.

Most of them have more leads than they can handle, so they're willing to part ways with a large percentage of that revenue to have someone come in and simply enroll those leads into their services over a zoom call.

So basically – you take their pre-warmed leads, spend a few hours a day on calls with them, and anyone that you enroll, you get a piece of that deal. Which can be thousands of dollars in some cases.

With Remote Closing, you get all the benefits of an online business. 

You can:

✅ Work from anywhere
✅ Travel the world
✅ Set your own schedule
✅ Do work that impacts people
✅ And get access to an unlimited income potential

Without having to spend months and thousands of dollars starting your own business.
```

---

### Social Proof

**Quick Outline:**

```
And by the way, we've helped over [X] [specific person] get [outcome]. Here are just a couple examples:

[NAME] was able to [result] in [timeframe]
[NAME] was able to [result] in [timeframe]

And hundreds more just like them.
```

---

### CTA (Call To Action)

**Structure:** CTA → Restate Key Points/Benefits → CTA Again

**Example:**

```
So if you want to [main benefit], here's what you need to do.

Click on the link below this ad, fill out a brief form, and then book a call with my team.

We'll break down our [Method Name] from A to Z so you can decide if it's the right thing to help you [main outcome].

Look, if you're [target audience qualifier], you HAVE to [key insight] to [desired result].

So click now and book a call with my team if you want to learn more about the [Method Name] and [compelling benefit]!
```
